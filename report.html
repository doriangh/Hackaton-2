Santa Claus Gift Selection Knowledge Model

The Santa Claus Gift Selection Knowledge Model is a conceptual model that helps Santa Claus choose the right gifts for children based on their interests and demographic profiles, as well as transportation and delivery methods based on the child's location, weather, and road conditions.

Entities

Child

A child is an individual who is eligible to receive gifts from Santa Claus. Each child has one or more interests and a demographic profile.

Hobby

A hobby is a particular activity or interest that a child enjoys.

Demographic

Demographic is a set of characteristics that describes a child, such as their age, gender, nationality and socio-economic status. This information can be used to help Santa Claus choose appropriate gifts for the child.

Toy

A toy is a plaything that is often given as a gift to children.

Instrument

A instrument is a device that is used to create art. It is often given as a gift to children who are interested in music.

Transportation

A transportation method is a means of getting from one place to another, such as by car, plane, or sleigh. Each transportation method has a transportation method type, which specifies the category of transportation, such as land, air, or water.

Weather

Weather is the state of the atmosphere at a particular time and place. It can impact the transportation and delivery methods that are available and suitable for a particular location. Each weather has a weather type, which specifies the category of weather, such as sunny, rainy, or snowy.

Road Condition

Road conditions are the physical state of a road, such as smooth, rough, or icy. They can impact the transportation and delivery methods that are available and suitable for a particular location. Each road condition has a road condition type, which specifies the category of road condition.

Properties

The Santa Claus Gift Selection Knowledge Model includes the following properties:

  • likes: Specifies the interests of a child
  • dislikes: Specifies the disinterest of a child
  • isFrom: Specifies the country from which a child is
  • hasGender: Specifies the gender of the child

    • These properties refer to the properties of the gift:
  • hasDimensions: Specifies the dimensions of the gift
  • transportedWith: Specifies the means of transportation this gift can be transported with
  • isUsedWhenWeatherIs: Specifies the type of weather needed to be able to enjoy this gift

    • These properties refer to the means of transportation:
  • runsOn: Specifies the fuel needed for the transportation method
  • doesNotRunOn: Specifies what fuel is not needeed for the transportation method
